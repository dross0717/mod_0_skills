How to become a Turing Student

1. Become a turing Student
  1.1 Apply to Turing
    1.1.1 Decide on Turing for coding school
      1.1.1.1 Research multiple coding schools
        1.1.1.1.1 Look into turing
        1.1.1.1.2 Look into Galvanize
      1.1.1.2 Determine which school fits needs
    1.1.2 Complete online application
      1.1.2.1 Fill out basic personal information
      1.1.2.2 Write out paragraph responses
      1.1.2.3 complete the practice LSAT portion
        1.1.2.3.1 Study LSAT Logic problems
  1.2 Interview for Turing
    1.2.1 Schedule Interview
      1.2.1.1 Decide in-person or video interview
      1.2.1.2 Choose a date and time for Interview
        1.2.1.2.1 Check calendar
        1.2.1.2.2 Tell interviewer your available dates/times
        1.2.1.2.3 Write selected date/time in your calendar
    1.2.2 Prep for interview
      1.2.2.1 Study LSAT Logic problems
      1.2.2.2 Prep for typical behavioral interview questions
        1.2.2.2.1 Prep response for "why do you want to go to Turing"
        1.2.2.2.2 Prep response for "tell me about a time..." questions
      1.2.2.3 Check that necessary interview tech works
        1.2.2.3.1 check internet connection
        1.2.2.3.2 check that google hangout works
    1.2.3 Complete Interview
      1.2.3.1 Show up on interview date/time
      1.2.3.2 Complete LSAT logic problems
      1.2.3.3 Complete behavioral interview questions
      1.2.3.4 ask any remaining questions
  1.3 Get money for tuition
    1.3.1 Save money
      1.3.1.1 sell car
      1.3.1.2 sell old laptop
      1.3.1.3 sell old clothing
    1.3.2 Ask parents for money
    1.3.3 Look into loan options
      1.3.3.1 research Sallie mae
      1.3.3.2 research Meritize
  1.4 Complete mod 0
    1.4.1 Show up for all classes
    1.4.2 Do all the homework
